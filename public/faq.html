Aziana FAQ

Answers for guests looking for sushi, Asian fusion dining, online ordering, reservations, catering and events at Aziana in Bobby's Marina, Philipsburg.

Where is Aziana located?

Aziana is located at Bobby's Marina, Juancho Yrausquin Boulevard #22, Philipsburg, Sint Maarten.

What kind of food does Aziana serve?

Aziana serves Asian fusion cuisine including sushi, sashimi, poke bowls, seafood, steaks, satay and Chinese-Indonesian specialties.

Can I order online?

Yes. Use the Order Online button on the Aziana website to start an online order.

Can I reserve a table?

Yes. Use the Reserve a Table button on the website, call the restaurant, or contact Aziana by WhatsApp.

Does Aziana handle catering or private events?

Yes. Aziana can help with birthdays, weddings, corporate evenings, group dinners and catering inquiries.
