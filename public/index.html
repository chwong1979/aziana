Our Story

A legacy continued.

In 1995, Molly and Mario opened Old Captain in Great Bay, Philipsburg, and quietly introduced sushi and Chinese-Indonesian cooking to an island that had seen neither.

The work continued through the restaurants that followed, and in 2018 it continued again at Bobby's Marina under the name Aziana.

To dine here is to step into that legacy and add a chapter to it.
